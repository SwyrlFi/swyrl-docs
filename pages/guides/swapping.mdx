# How To Swap

## Breaking Down the Trade Page

1. **Navigate to Trade:**  
   Click **Trade** on the top bar to open the swap interface.

![Swap 1](/swap1.png)

2. **Connect Wallet:**  
   Click your wallet address (e.g., `0x30...1A1f`) to connect and view balances.

3. **Select Token:**  
   Choosing the token you want to trade from the token list pop-up.

![Swap 4](/swap4.png)

4. **Enter Amount:**  
   Under **From**, type the amount (e.g., `1 MON`) or use **50% / Max**.

5. **View Rate:**  
   Shows conversion, e.g. `1 MON = 3.65 USDC`.

![Swap 3](/swap3.png)

6. **Check Price Impact:**  
   Example `0.12%` — lower means deeper liquidity.

7. **Minimum Received:**  
   Displays protected output, e.g. `3.63 USDC`.

8. **Slippage Tolerance:**  
   Default `0.5%`. Adjust via **⚙** for volatility.

9. **Show Chart:**  
   Toggle to view price and liquidity depth.

![Swap 2](/swap2.png)

10. **Execute Swap:**  
   Click **Swap**, confirm in wallet, and tokens update.

![Swap 5](/swap5.png)
